What we do

A safer campus
starts here

ScratchXI gives every member of the DUT community a direct line to campus security — from reporting an incident to tracking its resolution in real time.

🚨
Instant Incident Reporting
Students and staff can report incidents in seconds — with photo evidence, exact location, and severity level — directly from any device.
📡
Real-Time Alerts
Emergency broadcasts reach every connected user instantly. Live alert feeds update without page refresh across all dashboards.
🛡️
Security Task Management
Admin assigns incidents to available security personnel. Officers accept, investigate, and submit reports — all tracked end-to-end.
📊
Analytics & Hotspots
Visual dashboards reveal incident hotspots by campus and block, helping admin deploy security resources where they're needed most.
💬
Secure Campus Chat
Direct communication channel between campus users and security. Moderated, rate-limited, and fully logged for accountability.
🔒
Role-Based Access
Four distinct roles — Student, Staff, Security, and Admin — each with carefully scoped permissions and dedicated dashboards.
